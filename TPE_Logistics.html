TPE Logistics

Advanced Order & Container Management System for Efficient Delivery Operations

Project Overview

Business Challenge & Solution Approach

TPE Logistics, a company specializing in order and container tracking for tenants, required a comprehensive application to manage their complex delivery operations. The challenge was to create a unified system that could handle order processing, container tracking, tenant management, and delivery coordination in real-time. As the lead developer, I designed and implemented a robust logistics management platform that streamlines operations and ensures timely, accurate deliveries.

Key Features & System Architecture

Order Management System

Comprehensive order processing with automated status tracking, priority handling, and tenant-specific workflows for efficient order lifecycle management.

Tenant Management

Multi-tenant architecture supporting individual tenant dashboards, custom configurations, and isolated data management for secure operations.

Delivery Coordination

Intelligent routing algorithms, driver assignment, and optimized delivery scheduling to ensure timely and cost-effective deliveries.

Analytics Dashboard

Comprehensive reporting and analytics with real-time insights into delivery performance, order trends, and operational efficiency metrics.

Core System Components
